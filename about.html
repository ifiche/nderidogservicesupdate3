About Nderi Kennels

Elite canine excellence since 2017

Our Mission

At Nderi Kennels, we are dedicated to breeding, training, and providing exceptional protection dogs and canine services. Our mission is to create strong, reliable partnerships between dogs and their handlers through professional training and ethical breeding practices.

Founded in 2017 in Nairobi, we have grown to become one of Kenya's premier canine service providers, trusted by families, security firms, and individuals across the country.

Our approach combines modern training techniques with deep understanding of canine psychology, ensuring each dog reaches its full potential.
