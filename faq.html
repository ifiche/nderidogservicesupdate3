Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services

What breeds do you train for protection work?
We specialize in German Shepherds, Belgian Malinois, Rottweilers, and Dutch Shepherds. These breeds possess the ideal combination of intelligence, loyalty, courage, and physical capability for protection work. We also evaluate other breeds on a case-by-case basis.
How long does protection dog training take?
Our comprehensive protection program runs 10-14 weeks. This allows sufficient time for foundational obedience, scenario training, and proofing in various environments. Basic obedience takes 2 weeks, advanced obedience 6 weeks, and full protection work 10-14 weeks.
Do you offer boarding for all dog breeds?
Yes! We board all breeds, sizes, and temperaments. All boarding dogs must have up-to-date vaccinations (rabies, DHPP, and bordetella). We provide climate-controlled kennels, daily exercise, and individualized care based on each dog's needs.
What is the handler certification course?
Our 2-week intensive handler course certifies individuals to work professionally with protection and service dogs. The curriculum covers canine psychology, handling techniques, legal framework, emergency procedures, and practical hands-on training. Graduates receive industry-recognized certification.
Can I visit the kennels before booking?
Absolutely. We welcome prospective clients to schedule a tour of our facilities. Please call ahead to arrange a visit between 9am and 4pm, Monday through Saturday. This allows you to meet our trainers, see our training methods, and ensure our facility meets your expectations.
Do you sell trained protection dogs?
Yes, we offer fully trained protection dogs from our breeding program or select outside dogs. Each dog comes with a health guarantee, training records, and post-sale support. We match dogs to clients based on specific needs, family situation, and experience level.
What vaccinations are required for boarding?
All boarding dogs must have current rabies, DHPP (distemper, hepatitis, parainfluenza, parvovirus), and bordetella (kennel cough) vaccinations. Please bring vaccination records at check-in. We prioritize the health and safety of all dogs in our care.
Do you offer payment plans?
Yes, for programs exceeding 4 weeks we offer flexible payment plans with a 50% deposit required to secure your spot, with the balance split across the training duration. Contact us to discuss options tailored to your situation.
Do you train puppies?
Yes, we offer puppy training starting from 8 weeks old. Our puppy program focuses on socialization, basic obedience, potty training, and establishing good behaviors early. Early training is crucial for developing a well-adjusted adult dog.
Do you serve clients outside Nairobi?
Yes, we serve clients across Kenya, including Kiambu, Mombasa, Nakuru, Eldoret, Thika, and other regions. For clients outside Nairobi, we offer transportation arrangements for dogs and remote consultation options. Contact us to discuss your location.
