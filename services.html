Our Services

Comprehensive canine solutions for every need

Protection Dog Training

Our elite protection training program develops dogs that are confident, controlled, and capable of protecting their handlers and property. We work with various breeds including German Shepherds, Belgian Malinois, and Rottweilers. Each dog undergoes rigorous training to ensure reliability in real-world situations.

  • Personal protection
  • Property guarding
  • Threat assessment
  • Controlled aggression
  • Family integration
  • Vehicle protection

Handler Certification

Professional training for individuals who want to work with protection and service dogs. Our certification program covers canine psychology, handling techniques, legal aspects, and emergency procedures. Graduates receive industry-recognized certification.

  • 2-week intensive course
  • Practical hands-on training
  • Industry-recognized certification
  • Ongoing support
  • Legal framework training
  • Emergency procedures

Breeding Program

We maintain a selective breeding program focused on producing dogs with exceptional temperament, health, and working ability. All breeding dogs are health tested and selected for optimal genetic traits. Puppies are socialized from birth.

  • Premium bloodlines
  • Health guarantee
  • Early socialization
  • Lifetime support
  • Genetic testing
  • Veterinary certified

Boarding & Care

Safe, comfortable boarding facilities with professional supervision. Each dog receives daily exercise, proper nutrition, and individualized attention. Our climate-controlled kennels ensure your dog's comfort year-round.

  • Climate-controlled kennels
  • Daily exercise sessions
  • Veterinary supervision
  • 24/7 security
  • Custom feeding plans
  • Playtime activities

Professional Grooming

Full-service grooming by experienced professionals. We cater to all breeds and coat types using premium products. Regular grooming maintains your dog's health and appearance while providing a relaxing experience.

  • Bathing & drying
  • Haircuts & styling
  • Nail trimming
  • Teeth cleaning
  • Ear cleaning
  • De-shedding treatment
